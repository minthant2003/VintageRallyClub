Who we are...


Vintage Rally Club of UK is the home of classic navigational rallying in Europe. We aim to provide an association to promote the enjoyment of classic car rallies and recreational use of classic cars in rallies, to hold social gatherings for members and friends and to promote classic rally cars in displays.

What we do...


We support a broad family of enthusiasts who want to explore the Europe in historic and classic vehicles. We organise rallies which are competitive, with significant off-road secions and challenge us through all the countries in Europe.
