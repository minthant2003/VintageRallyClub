Urban Rally

Urban Rally held by Vintage Rally Club is the 10th rally in 2022 across five beautiful and classic countries in Europe: Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Denmark and France. The Rally aims to provide an association to promote the enjoyment of classic car rallies and to hold social gatherings for members and friends.
